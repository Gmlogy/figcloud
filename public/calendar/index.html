Fig Cloud for Figphone

Secure calendar sync between your Figphone and the web

Fig Cloud is a companion service for Figphone users. It lets you connect your Google Calendar account so your events can stay in sync across your Fig device and your web dashboard.

Login / Connect Calendar → Privacy Policy
What Fig Cloud does
Connects your Google Calendar so you can authorize sync from your computer.
Reads and writes calendar events only to keep your calendar data updated between devices.
Works for Figphone users who want a secure cross-device calendar experience.
User-initiated access only — calendar permissions are requested only when you choose to connect your account.

Purpose of the app

Fig Cloud is designed to help Figphone users manage and synchronize calendar events between their phone and the web in a secure and simple way.

Google Calendar access

Fig Cloud requests Google Calendar access to read and write events needed for two-way synchronization across the user’s connected Fig experience.

Who this is for

This service is intended for Figphone users who want to connect Google Calendar and keep their schedules updated across supported Fig services.

How it works

Connect your device, approve access, and keep events in sync

To get started, open the connection page, enter the verification code shown on your Figphone, and sign in with Google on your computer. Once approved, Fig Cloud uses the granted calendar permission to help keep your events synchronized across your connected device experience.
